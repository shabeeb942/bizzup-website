  • The RHQ Program is a joint initiative by the Ministry of Investment and the Royal Commission for Riyadh City, created as part of Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 and the National Investment Strategy.

  • The program is designed to enable and facilitate the ambitious growth plans of participating organizations in the region.

    • Saudi Arabia presents a multitude of benefits for companies locating their RHQ in the country

    Strategic Location

    Advantageous location to access a large market. Enhancement of regional operations and expansion of presence in the broader region.

    Business-friendly environment

    An investment ecosystem that promotes regulatory transparency, solid infrastructure, and talent attraction, enabling predictability in RHQ investments.

    Specially designed incentives

    Specially designed incentives for establishing RHQs including human capital incentives, tax incentives, and special hand-holding end to end support.

    Game-changing opportunities

    Dynamic business landscape and access to unparalleled opportunities for strategic growth across sectors.

    Strategic Initiatives

    Initiatives that enable regional expansion and provide investors with a competitive edge. Initiatives include the (GSCRI) and the Special Economic Zones (SEZ).

    RHQ Incentives

    We offer specially designed incentives such as human capital incentives, tax incentives, and special handholding support services.

    10-year Saudization Exemption

    Exemption from complying with Saudization percentage requirements for 10 years for the RHQ entity.

    Visa Limit Exemption

    250 visas are allocated automatically from day 1 of the RHQ license being granted.

    Waiver of Professional Accreditation

    The RHQ employees are exempted from professional accreditation requirements applicable in the Kingdom if they are duly accredited in the country of origin, except of Engineering and Medical certification.

    Expat Work Visas for Professions Restricted to Saudi Nationals

    RHQ employees benefit from an accelerated issuance of work visas for professions normally restricted to Saudi nationals.

    Spouse work permit and Dependent age extension to 25 years old

    Dependents of RHQ employees are permitted to work and apply for jobs. The maximum age for residency of male dependents of RHQ employees is extended to 25 years old; no age restriction for single female dependents.

    A 30-years 0% Withholding Tax

    Withholding tax payments to non-resident related parties, Withholding tax (‘’WHT’’) on dividend payments from RHQ to its foreign parent entity(ies), Withholding tax for payments to non-resident unrelated parties for services necessary to carry out RHQ activities

    Eligibility for Premium Residency

    Premium residency for RHQ’s executives subject to the Eligibility Premium Residency Program

    RHQ Considerations

    Presence

    • An RHQ maintains a physical office presence and acts as the center of administrative power within the region.

    Operations

    • The MNC should commence RHQ operations within six months of receiving the license. Additionally, it is expected to initiate three optional activities, chosen within the first year of operating under the granted license.
    • The RHQ should not directly conduct commercial operations that generate revenue other than the activities stipulated in the RHQ license. All commercial activities will be conducted by the company affiliates possessing a Service License, Commercial License, Industrial License, or any relevant License for commercial operations obtained from MISA.

    Legal Status

    • Any RHQ under the RHQ Program is a unit of a multinational company, duly established under the laws of Saudi Arabia.
    • The RHQ operates as a registered foreign company or branch, with a separate legal personality within the Kingdom.

    Employees

    • The RHQ should employ a minimum of 15 full-time employees, including three top executives, such as CEO, CFO, Executive Director and Vice President within one year of obtaining the RHQ License.
